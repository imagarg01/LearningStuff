## Map the Value Chain

```text
You are an industry analyst for <name of company>, create a 10 page value chain report covering:

1. Who pays whom and how money flows.

2. Player archetypes for each layer (with examples)

3. Where margin concentrates and why

4. Switching costs and local-in mechanism

5. Control points (Standard, licenses, data)

6. Control Structures and incentive misalignments

7. Single point of failure

8. What layer holds durable power

Use Official filing. Focus on mechanism, not valuation. Include a table: Layer | Player Type | Revenue Model | Moat | Power Trend
```

**Specific Insights you will uncover:**

- Why certain players can raise prices (and others cannot)
- Which bottlenecks creates 40%+ margin
- Where new entrants get blocked by regulation or standard
- What contract terms lock in customers
- Who controls the data flows that matter

## Find Growth and Margin Levers

```text
You are financial analyst for <company name> analyze:

1. Growth = Volume *Price* Mix *New Products* Geography
2. Margin Tree = Gross -> Operating -> Cash (Show all levers)
3. Unit economics = price, cost, contribution, CAC, LTV, payback, retention
4. Cyclicality: What demand drivers swing revenue
5. Sensitivities: if [metrics] moves x% what happens to margin?
6. 8-10 leading indicators with thresholds to watch
Use official filings, transcripts, investor presentations. Focus on mechanism, not valuation. Show mechanism only. Include sector specific KPIs(for payments: take rate, authorization rate, etc; for SaaS: NRR, GRR).
```

## Decode the Strategy

```text
You are a strategy analyst for <company name> create a 10 page strategy report covering:
1. unit economics: today vs 5-year target
2. Where to play/ How to win framework
3. Moat renewal: what deepens the advantage?
4. Growth Vectors: new customers, wallet share, new products, new geographies
5. Capital allocation: ROIC, reinvestment, M&A strategy
6. What breaks at scale vs what tightens
7. Risk heat map with concrete failure modes
8. 12-quarter roadmap with pass/fail milestones
Use official filings, transcripts, investor presentations. Focus on mechanism, not valuation. Show mechanism only Include margin tree, KPI dashboard, scenario analysis. Use 10-Ks and investor decks. Mechanism over narrative.
```
